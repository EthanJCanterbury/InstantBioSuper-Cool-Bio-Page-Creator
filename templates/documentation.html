Getting Started

Bio Creator allows you to create and share customizable bio pages with your own personalized URL. Whether you're a content creator, professional, or just someone who wants a simple way to share information about yourself, Bio Creator makes it easy.

What is a Bio Page?

A bio page is a single webpage that contains information about you, including:

  • Your name and profile picture
  • A short bio or description
  • Links to your social media accounts
  • Custom styling to match your personal brand

Creating Your Bio

To create a new bio page:

  1. Click the Create Your Bio button on the homepage or navigate to /create
  2. Fill out the form with your details:
    • Bio URL: This will be your custom URL (alphanumeric characters only)
    • Your Name: Your full name or the name you want to display
    • Tagline: A short phrase describing what you do
    • Bio: A longer description about yourself
    • Profile Image: Upload a profile picture
    • Theme: Choose one of our pre-made themes
  3. Add your social media links if desired
  4. Click Create Bio to save your page

Editing Your Bio

You can edit your bio page at any time:

  1. Navigate to your bio page at http://your-url/your-bio-name
  2. Click the Edit link at the bottom of the page
  3. Make your changes in the form
  4. Click Update Bio to save your changes

Customizing Themes

Bio Creator offers several built-in themes to match your style:

  • Default: A clean, professional look
  • Dark: A sleek dark mode theme
  • Light: A bright, minimal look
  • Colorful: A vibrant, attention-grabbing theme
  • Minimal: A stripped-down, typography-focused design

To change your theme, edit your bio page and select a different theme from the dropdown menu.

Using Custom CSS

For advanced users, you can add custom CSS to completely personalize your bio page's appearance:

  1. Edit your bio page
  2. Scroll down to the Custom CSS section
  3. Add your CSS code in the text area

Example custom CSS:

.bio-card {
  background: linear-gradient(135deg, #6e8efb, #a777e3);
  color: white;
}

.name {
  font-family: 'Georgia', serif;
  text-transform: uppercase;
  letter-spacing: 2px;
}

Sharing Your Bio

Once your bio is created, you can share it using your custom URL:

http://your-url/your-bio-name

Consider adding this link to your:

  • Social media profiles
  • Email signature
  • Business card
  • Resume or CV

Using the API

Bio Creator offers a comprehensive API for developers who want to programmatically create and manage bio pages. Visit our API Documentation for complete details.

Common API Use Cases:

  • Creating bio pages for users of your application
  • Integrating bio creation into your product workflow
  • Building tools that leverage the Bio Creator platform

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bio Creator free to use?

Yes, Bio Creator is completely free to use for creating and sharing your personal bio page.

Can I have multiple bio pages?

Yes, you can create as many unique bio pages as you want, each with its own URL.

Can I delete my bio page?

Yes, you can delete your bio page using the API with a DELETE request to /api/bio/your-bio-name.

Do bio pages support media embeds?

Currently, bio pages support profile images and links. Media embeds may be added in future updates.

Can I use my own domain name?

Custom domain support is not available at this time, but may be added in the future.
